Attribute definitions and systems.

About Attributes

Attributes are Temple’s translation of a Bevy component into a manifest-defined space for use with sprites (see [crate::sprite]). In most scenario’s, they are simply tags to apply a system, but attributes additionally have the ability to take parameters to generalize their functionality.
